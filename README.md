# ONA Dashboard

Modern organisations generate enormous volumes of communication data — emails, meetings, messages — yet most leaders rely on org charts that show reporting lines, not real relationships. The signal is buried in the noise: who actually bridges teams, which departments are siloed, who is overloaded or overlooked. ONA Dashboard surfaces those patterns instantly.

Upload a CSV of interactions (From, To, Date, Department), select your columns, and hit Generate. Within seconds you see your organisation as a live network — clusters, bridges, isolated individuals, and cross-functional flows — across eight interactive chart types with timeline playback and AI-powered analysis.

## Why ONA Dashboard

- **Eight chart types** — Network Graph, Sankey, Venn, Heatmap, Radar, Box/Violin, Chord, Time Series
- **Privacy first** — all processing happens in the browser; your data never leaves your machine
- **Timeline playback** — animate the network over time to watch relationships evolve
- **AI assistant** — ask questions about your network in plain language
- **Exportable** — download any chart as a JPG or self-contained interactive HTML
- **No installation** — runs in any modern browser, no build step required
